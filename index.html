My Web Design Pathway

My specialties include HTML, CSS, and Photoshop, and while I'm not as proficient in Javascript, I'm eager to learn more. I take pride in my work and always strives to exceed my client's expectations when being commissioned for a project. Orb in Landscape

As the piece unfolds, the orb takes on a life of its own, seemingly imbued with the power to manipulate the very fabric of its surroundings. With a deft twist and turn, the orb begins to warp the landscape, distorting it in mesmerizing ways and captivating the viewer's imagination. Through skillful use of color, form, and composition, this piece invites us on a journey of visual exploration, where the boundaries between reality and fantasy become delightfully blurred.

Fish in Lightbulb

This piece is a unique and curious creation that features a lightbulb housing two lively goldfish. However, this unconventional sight is not the only surprise that this piece has to offer. Upon closer inspection, one can spot a tiny Blahaj plush nestled among the aquatic inhabitants of the lightbulb. This unexpected detail adds a whimsical element to the artwork, infusing it with a sense of fun and lightheartedness. Overall, this piece is a delightful and charming exploration of the unexpected and the imaginative.

Baby Chase: The Goat

"Baby Chase: The Goat" is a digital art piece that depicts the artist's younger sibling, Chase. In the artwork, Chase is surrounded by a neon purple triangle that appears to be glowing. The triangle is an abstract representation of the artist's love and protection for their sibling. The use of vibrant colors and bold lines conveys a sense of energy and playfulness, reflecting the spirit of youth.

Javascript Booklist

This is one of the only javascript websites that I have developed. The website allows users to manage a list of book titles, including adding new titles, deleting existing titles, and hiding the entire list. The website's functionality is achieved through the use of Javascript, which allows for dynamic, client-side interactivity without the need for server-side processing.
